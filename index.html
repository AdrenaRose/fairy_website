Fairy

Faerie ~ Sprite ~ Pixie ~ Fae


A mythical being of folklore, fairies are described as winged, magical creatures who usually have a close relationship with nature and humans.

While the term fairy goes back only to the middle ages in Europe, these beings are described in various forms throughout history in Sanskrit, Greek mythology, Arabic mythology, and stories told by Samoans and indigenous Americans.
